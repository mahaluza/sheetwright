# Sheetwright — Privacy Policy

Sheetwright is an open-source skill that lets Claude edit your Google Sheets and Drive through
Google's official API, using OAuth credentials that you create and that stay on your own machine.

- **No data collection.** Sheetwright has no servers and no analytics. It does not read, store, or
  transmit your conversations, files, or spreadsheet contents anywhere other than directly between
  your environment and Google's APIs, to perform the actions you request.
- **Credentials.** Your Google OAuth credentials (client id/secret and refresh token) are stored
  locally in a private folder you control. They are never sent to the author or any third party.
  You can revoke access anytime at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.
- **Third parties.** The only external service Sheetwright talks to is Google (Sheets & Drive APIs),
  acting on your behalf with the access you granted.
- **Contact.** Report issues or questions via this repository's Issues page:
  https://github.com/mahaluza/sheetwright/issues
